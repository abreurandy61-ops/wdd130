Santo Domingo

Santo Domingo is the capital and largest city of the Dominican Republic, located on the southern coast along the Caribbean Sea. Founded in 1496, it is the oldest continuously inhabited European city in the Americas and home to the Colonial Zone, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The city is a major cultural, political, and economic hub, known for its rich history, vibrant music and nightlife, and a mix of modern urban life with well-preserved colonial architecture.
